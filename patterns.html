How EduAI Detects Learning Patterns
Our AI analyzes 12+ behavioral signals per student: submission timing, resource access patterns, quiz re-attempt frequency, peer collaboration requests, video watch time, note-taking style, engagement dips/peaks, and assessment response patterns. Using a multi-layer classification model, students are assigned to one of 6 primary learning archetypes — enabling educators to deliver personalized content, pacing, and support.
